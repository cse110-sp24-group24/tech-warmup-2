# User Stories: Entertainment Persona
## Curious Child
### User Story: I am a kid from vegas, always seeing the grown ups gamble, I want to learn and partake in the fun, but I will only stay interested if the game is interactive, catches my attention, and gives me reasons to come back and keep playing


## Acceptance Criteria:

- Interactive Features and minigames  to catch my attention more than just spinning a lever
- Earning a daily bonus to keep me coming back
- A competitive leaderboard so that I can compete with friends
