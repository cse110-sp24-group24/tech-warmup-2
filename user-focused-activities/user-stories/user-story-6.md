# User Stories: Gambaling Persona
## Coming of Age Hero
### User Story: I am a 18 year old who just got old enough to gamble, and want to play online slot machines. I want some kind of bonus’s to keep the excitement, and lots of big numbers to support my big dreams. I also want redemption opportunities if i lose all my money, such as a free 5x autospin round after l lost all my money.


## Acceptance Criteria:

- Deposit/sign up bonus to convince me to pick this platform
- Chances to win big (could be very rare, but have big numbers like up to 1000x)
- Redemption auto spin if I run out of money to keep my inner hope
