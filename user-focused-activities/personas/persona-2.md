# User Persona 2: The Casual Entertainer

**Name:** Elena Rostova  
**Age:** 45  
**Occupation:** Office Manager / Casual Mobile Gamer  

### Background
Elena plays digital slots and casino games strictly to unwind after a long day or while watching TV. She doesn't know or care what "RNG" or "RTP" means. To her, the app is a form of entertainment. She has a low tolerance for risk and prefers low-volatility machines. She wants to win small amounts frequently so she can keep playing for a long time without depleting her token stash. 

### Wants & Needs
* **Entertainment Value:** She loves the "casino experience." She wants exciting animations, sound effects, and celebratory graphics when she wins.
* **Low Stakes:** She wants the ability to bet the absolute minimum (ex. 1 token) so she can stretch her session out for an hour.
* **Near-Miss Excitement:** She enjoys the suspense of watching the reels slow down one by one, especially when she almost hits a jackpot.
* **Aesthetic & Theme:** She prefers machines with fun graphics, bright colors, and perhaps a loose theme or mini-game feature.

### Doesn't Want / Doesn't Need
* **Spreadsheet UI:** She doesn't want the game to look like a stock trading app. If it’s purely numbers and fast spins without flair, she will get bored.
* **Rapid Depletion:** She hates hitting a "Max Bet" button by accident and losing half her tokens in one second. 
* **Complex Math:** She doesn't want to read a complicated paytable; she just wants the game to clearly highlight the winning lines on the screen when a spin finishes.

### Technical Literacy & Frustrations
* **Literacy:** Average. She knows how to navigate standard mobile apps but isn't a power user. 
* **Frustrations:** Small, unreadable text, accidental clicks that cost her tokens, and games that feel "dry" or lack celebratory feedback when she wins.

### Goal for our Software
If we are building this for Elena, our UI must include a visually pleasing layout, celebratory animations for winning states (even small wins), and safeguards (like a confirmation prompt on "Max Bet") to prevent accidental massive losses.
