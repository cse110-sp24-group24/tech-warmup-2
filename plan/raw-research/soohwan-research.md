# Soohwan’s Research - Slot Machine

## 1. Core Features
- **Spin Button** – starts the game  
- **Reels** – 3 to 5 rotating columns  
- **Paylines** – lines that determine winning combinations  
- **Balance / Credits** – shows current money or points  
- **Bet Controls** – allows users to adjust bet amount  

## 2. Game Mechanics
- **Auto Spin** – automatically spins multiple times  
- **Bonus Round** – special mini-game triggered by symbols  
- **Free Spins** – free rounds without cost  
- **Multiplier** – increases winnings  
- **Jackpot** – large reward system  

## 3. Advanced Features
- **Daily Rewards** – login bonuses  
- **Leaderboard** – ranking system  
- **Shop / Items** – purchase upgrades or boosts  
- **Settings** – sound and animation controls  

---

## 4. Common Slot Machine Terminology
- **Reel** – the spinning column  
- **Spin** – starting the game  
- **Payline** – winning line  
- **Symbol** – icons (cherry, 7, BAR, etc.)  
- **Wild** – substitutes for other symbols  
- **Scatter** – triggers bonus features  
- **Jackpot** – highest reward  
- **Multiplier** – increases payout
