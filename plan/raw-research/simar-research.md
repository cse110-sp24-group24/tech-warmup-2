# Simar’s Research

### Gameplay ideas:

* Hallucinations:   
  * The UI occasionally shows the user a “win” but then corrects itself, basically displaying consistent results   
  * follows the theme of mocking AI  
* Many small wins & “almost wins”  
  * psychological effect on the user, follows the addictive nature of gambling  
* upgrade/economy system  
  * temporary multipliers, power-ups (bonus spins, etc.), shops/currency system  
* balance/credit system  
  * tracks player’s tokens (cost per spin & results)

### UI/UX

* Flashy visuals with strong feedback (confetti, flashing indicators, screen shake, animated number counters)  
* Distinct sound design:  
* Win sounds vs near-win sounds  
* Loss feedback can be humorous or exaggerated (UI dimming, messages)  
* The slot reels should remain the main visual focus of the screen  
* Potential themes:  
* AI dystopia  
* Hacker terminal  
* Minimalist “AI dashboard”

### Feedback/Interaction

* Model delays revealing results  
  * displays “thinking…” (builds anticipation)  
* Maybe can include a glitch effect for hallucinations during gameplay  
  * Text flicker, UI distortion, etc.  
* Visual indicators for big wins  
  * Visual emphasis for near-wins as well

### Slot Machine Terms to Consider:

* Reels, Symbols  
* Paylines  
  * determine how symbols line up for a win  
* Paytable  
  * the mapping of symbol combinations, controls reward structure  
* RNG (random number generator)  
  * ensures randomness of the gameplay  
* RTP (return to player)  
  * percent of money retired to players over time  
* Volatility  
  * low \= freq small wins  
  * high \= rare big wins  
  * shapes player experience
